OrnithoClue API

GET /birds

Retrieve data from all the birds.

GET /birds/:name

Retrieve data specific birds by their name.

POST /saves

Adds a username and the level they are currently in.

PUT /saves/:username

Updates the level for a specific user.

DELETE /saves/:username

Deletes a profile of a specific user and their savedata.

GET /saves/:username

Get the username and level number from a specific user.
